I am an incoming Master's student in Computer Science at Georgia Tech.
I am currently a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research where I focus on leveraging large language models (LLMs) across various use cases, including assisted coding, education, and improving speaking skills. My research interests lie in programming languages, formal methods, accessibility and HCI.
